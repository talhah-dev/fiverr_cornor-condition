Corner Condition

Studio Statement

Intersecting media, political critique, and collective action.

CORNER CONDITION is a multi-valent studio organized by artists Haley Castro and Evan Ho. Their work spans across media to include video, 2D art, digital architecture, sound and scores, sculpture, and performance.

"CORNER CONDITION" is their title. It describes a flux-state of plural media: intersecting, overlapping, and intercoursing. The practice exists in the spaces where forms collide and transform one another.

Their projects criticize labour exploitation and gendered power distribution under techno-capitalism. This work appropriates eroticism and sensuality to illuminate absurdity and contradiction inherent to privatized and corporate space.

Through audience engagement, theoretical architecture, technological research, and media exploration, this collaboration is a collective effort to deconstruct contemporary power relations and take action.
