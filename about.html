ABOUT

CORNER CONDITION IS A MULTI-VALENT STUDIO

ORGANIZED BY ARTISTS HALEY CASTRO

AND EVAN HO.

OUR WORK SPANS ACROSS MEDIA TO INCLUDE

VIDEO, 2D ART, DIGITAL ARCHITECTURE,

SOUND+SCORES, SCULPTURE, AND PERFORMANCE.

“CORNER CONDITION” IS OUR TITLE. IT DESCRIBES

A FLUX-STATE OF PLURAL MEDIA; INTERSECTING,

OVERLAPPING, INTERCOURSING.

OUR PROJECTS CRITICIZE LABOUR EXPLOITATION

AND GENDERED POWER DISTRIBUTION UNDER

TECHNO-CAPITALISM. THIS WORK APPROPRIATES

EROTICISM AND SENSUALITY TO ILLUMINATE

ABSURDITY AND CONTRADICTION INHERENT TO

PRIVATIZED/CORPORATE SPACE.

THROUGH AUDIENCE ENGAGEMENT, THEORETICAL

ARCHITECTURE, TECHNOLOGICAL RESEARCH

AND MEDIA EXPLORATION, THIS COLLABORATION

IS OUR COLLECTIVE EFFORT TO DECONSTRUCT

CONTEMPORARY POWER RELATIONS AND TAKE

ACTION.
