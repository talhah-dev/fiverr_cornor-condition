Corner Condition

About

Multivalent electronic music duo CORNER CONDITION is composed of California-born artists Haley Castro and Evan Ho. As their name suggests, the music evokes a flux-state drama, combining gabber, hardcore, and EBM inspired beats with politically-charged lyrics and jazz guitar solos. Appropriating high fashion and commercialized eroticism are just two vehicles of many that highlight Corner Condition's critique of contemporary power relations; a caricature of The Neoliberal Dream is thus extruded. Corner Condition's latest single release, entitled HUNDRED STRANGERS, precedes a debut album release planned for mid-2025.
